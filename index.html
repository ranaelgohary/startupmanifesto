KICK-OFF EVENT

The Startup Manifesto was kicked off successfully on September 26th, 2017 - we came together under one roof and behind one document to initiate a reform movement that surely will take the entrepreneurship ecosystem in Egypt to the next level.



We were honored to welcome representatives from Algebra Ventures, A15, the (former) Social Fund for Development, Flat6Labs, the World Bank, Shehata Attorney’s at Law, Levari LLP, PWC, AUC Venture Lab, FEBS Business Incubator, CIB, BUE, Endeavour, Him Angel, NGage, amongst others. Furthermore, we were delighted to have with us the founders / CEOs of Mumm, ABnG, Trendak, El Makinah and Chaino.



Topics discussed during the evening included:

● Need for more Collaboration
● Lack of Data
● Seed Funds
● Infrastructure
● Brain Drain
● Entrepreneurship Education
● Excessive Regulations
● Engaging the Government
● Know How Gaps and Team Building
● Accessibility/ Availability of Business Needs
● Legalities
● Potential Growth
● Variations of Funding Resources
● Bureaucracy
● Entrepreneurial Culture and Public Perception
● Role Model Startups

Participants emphasized that the time is now to transform the current entrepreneurial scene to an open and collaborative entrepreneurial ecosystem by strengthening connections among different stakeholders. Overall, the group clearly expressed the wish to take the discussion further.



Next steps include deep dives with key persons in the ecosystem on a one-on-one basis as well as the launch of a crowdfunding campaign.

If you want to support the Manifesto and become part of this group of changemakers, please drop an email at tamara@riseup.co
